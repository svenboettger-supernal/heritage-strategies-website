The Heritage Book is the firm's proprietary methodology for representing, refreshing, and acting on a family's full estate picture. It begins with a complete asset inventory across every entity and account and ends with a living set of artifacts the family, the trustees, and the attorneys can all read from the same page. Each section is owned, dated, and refreshed on a schedule the family controls.
