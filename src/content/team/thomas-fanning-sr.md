Mr. Fanning, founder and Co-Managing Partner of Heritage Strategies LLC, is widely recognized as a leading expert in the field of estate and business continuity planning, with more than three decades of experience advising ultra-affluent families on the preservation and transfer of generational wealth.

After graduating from Fairfield University in 1978, he began his career in the Individual Financial Distribution division of CIGNA, a $60 billion financial services organization. In 1990 he partnered with former CIGNA colleagues to establish a national life insurance brokerage firm serving prominent families. That partnership was acquired by National Financial Partners (NYSE) in 2002, where he served as President until 2009. He then reacquired his practice and founded Heritage Strategies.

Beyond Heritage, Mr. Fanning is Co-Managing Partner of Heritage Solutions LLC and F&F Ventures LLC; founder and board member of Gold Coast Bank; Chair of the Charles F. Dolan School of Business Advisory Council at Fairfield University; Vice Chairman of the Foundation Board of St. Francis Hospital; a member of the Chairman's Committee for SCO Family of Services; and an advisory board member of White Oak Partners. He lectures regularly for corporations, insurance companies, and accounting and law firms on estate planning and business strategy.
