Mr. Halvey joined Heritage in 2008 and is currently Managing Director of Insurance and Case Design. He earned a bachelor's degree from Stony Brook University with a double major in Economics and Business Management, and holds the CFP®, ChFC®, and CLU® designations.

Chris owns the insurance and case-design feedback loop across every Heritage Book engagement, working hand-in-hand with the firm's analysts and the families' trusted attorneys, accountants, and trustees to translate complex estate structures into actionable case design.
