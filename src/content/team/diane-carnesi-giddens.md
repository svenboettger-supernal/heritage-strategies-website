Ms. Carnesi Giddens is Director of Underwriting for Heritage Strategies, responsible for underwriting management across the firm's case-design pipeline. Her career in life insurance began in 2002, and she is a Nassau Community College graduate with an Associate degree. She is a Notary Public and resides in Oyster Bay, New York.

Diane partners with the firm's carriers and reinsurance relationships to navigate the underwriting nuances that arise on large, complex placements for high-net-worth families.
